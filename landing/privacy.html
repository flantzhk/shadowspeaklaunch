1. Introduction

Welcome to ShadowSpeak. We build tools that help people learn Cantonese and Mandarin through the shadowing method — listening to native audio, recording yourself, and getting real-time pronunciation feedback.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you use ShadowSpeak, why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data.

This app is operated by Colour Code Productions Ltd, based in Hong Kong ("we", "us", "our"). You can reach us at hello@shadowspeak.app.

This policy applies to the ShadowSpeak progressive web app (PWA) and any associated iOS app. By creating an account or using the app, you confirm that you've read and understood this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for login, account recovery, and (with your consent) product communications
  • Display name — if provided during registration or via Google sign-in
  • Authentication method — whether you registered via email/password or Google OAuth

This data is stored in Firebase Authentication (Google).

2.2 Audio recordings

ShadowSpeak's core feature is pronunciation practice. When you use any practice mode, your device's microphone captures your voice.

Your audio is processed in real time and is not stored by us. Here is exactly what happens: your device records a short audio clip, that audio is sent to cantonese.ai for real-time scoring via an encrypted API call, cantonese.ai returns a pronunciation score, and the audio itself is discarded. We do not build audio profiles. We do not store voice samples. The only output we retain is your score.

cantonese.ai's handling of audio data is governed by their own privacy policy. We have contractually required that they not store or use your audio data beyond immediate processing.

2.3 Usage and progress data

We store data about your learning activity so your progress is saved and you can continue across devices. This includes:

  • Practice sessions completed, lesson scores, and pronunciation scores
  • XP (experience points), streaks, and achievements unlocked
  • Practice mode preferences and settings
  • Time and date of sessions

This data is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB) and may be synced to Firebase Firestore for cross-device continuity.

2.4 Payment data

We do not store your payment card details. Payment is handled entirely by Stripe (web) and Apple In-App Purchase via RevenueCat (iOS). What we receive from these processors: subscription status, plan type, and transaction identifiers.

2.5 Analytics data

We use Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how the app is used. This includes device type, operating system, app version, feature usage (anonymised), and crash reports. Firebase Analytics data is associated with a randomly generated device identifier, not directly with your name or email address.

6. Your Rights

6.1 GDPR rights (EU residents)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your personal data (or do it in-app via Settings → Delete Account)
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data
  • Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@shadowspeak.app. We will respond within 30 days.

6.2 UK GDPR rights (UK residents)

UK residents have the same rights as outlined in section 6.1 under the UK GDPR. The UK supervisory authority is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk.

6.3 Hong Kong PDPO rights (HK residents)

Under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486) of Hong Kong, you have the right to access your personal data, request corrections, and be informed of how your data is used. To exercise these rights, email hello@shadowspeak.app. We will acknowledge your request within 10 days and respond within 40 days.

The HK Privacy Commissioner for Personal Data: pcpd.org.hk.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The ShadowSpeak web app may use cookies for session management (keeping you logged in — essential, cannot be disabled) and analytics (Firebase Analytics — can be opted out via your device settings).

Your practice progress, scores, XP, and streaks are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data does not leave your device unless you are logged in and sync is active. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser data.

8. Marketing Communications

We only send marketing emails if you have explicitly opted in. To opt out at any time, click the "Unsubscribe" link in any marketing email, or email hello@shadowspeak.app with the subject "Unsubscribe". Opting out does not affect transactional emails (e.g. password reset, subscription confirmation).

9. Children

ShadowSpeak is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact hello@shadowspeak.app and we will delete the account promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy
