Users never see or handle API keys. All API calls route through

This means: no API key setup screen, no key in client code,
no key in IndexedDB. Users just sign up and start learning.

How it works

1
Browse real HK moments
Topics like wet market bargaining, school pickup, and cha chaan teng orders. Not textbook dialogues nobody uses.
2
Save what matters to you
Build a personal library of phrases you actually need. Your daily lesson is built from this library.
3
Press play and speak
Shadow native speakers for 10, 20, or 30 minutes. Listen. Repeat. Get scored. That's your Cantonese practice, done.

Why not the other apps?

Duolingo doesn't offer Cantonese. Neither does Babbel, Rosetta Stone, or Busuu. The apps that do exist focus on reading and writing. You don't need that to order milk tea.

Other apps
Match tiles
Drag words into boxes. Feels productive. Doesn't get you speaking.
Other apps
Teach characters
Great for literacy. Useless when the taxi driver asks where you're going.
ShadowSpeak
